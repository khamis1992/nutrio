# Spotify Design Patterns

## Content-First
- Near-black immersive cocoon (#121212-#1f1f1f)
- UI recedes so content (album art, playlists) glows
- Only color comes from Spotify Green (#1ed760) and album artwork

## Pill Geometry
- Primary buttons: 500px-9999px radius (full pill)
- Search: 500px pill
- Circular play buttons: 50% radius
- Heavy shadows: rgba(0,0,0,0.5) 0px 8px 24px

## Single Accent
- Spotify Green (#1ed760) as singular brand accent
- Never decorative, always functional (play, active, CTA)

## Typography
- Bold/regular binary: 700 for emphasis, 400 for body
- Uppercase buttons with wide letter-spacing (1.4px-2px)
- Compact sizing: 10px-24px range — designed for scanning
