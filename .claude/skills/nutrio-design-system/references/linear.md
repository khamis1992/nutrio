# Linear Design Patterns

## Canvas
- #010102 — near-pure black with faint blue tint. Deepest dark surface.
- Four-step surface ladder: canvas → surface-1 → surface-2 → surface-3 → surface-4
- Hierarchy through surface lift, not shadow

## Single Accent
- Lavender-blue (#5e6ad2) used ONLY for: brand mark, primary CTA, focus ring, link emphasis
- No second chromatic color. No atmospheric gradients. No spotlight cards.

## Cards
- Surface-1 background, 1px hairline border
- 12px corners (not pill)
- Product UI screenshots dominate — marketing chrome is minimal

## Typography
- Aggressive negative tracking on display (-3.0px at 80px)
- Single voice from display to body
- Eyebrow uses positive tracking (+0.4px) for taxonomy

## Depth
- Carried by surface ladder + hairline borders
- Resists drop shadows on dark entirely
- Focus ring: 2px primary-focus outline at 50% opacity
