What is Total LTV?

LTV (Lifetime Value) = Total revenue a customer generates during their entire relationship with us.

Example: If a customer pays 3,800 QAR for a monthly subscription (Elite Plan), over 12 months, they would generate 9,600 QAR in total revenue from that customer.

Adjusted LTV (with 25% churn)

What is Payment Processing (2%)?
Payment processing is the small fee charged by banks and credit card companies (like Visa, Mastercard) for every transaction. For our platform, it's typically 1.5-3% of the the transaction amount. For example, a 50 QAR meal × 2% = 1 QAR (we pay 1 QAR to the payment processor).
