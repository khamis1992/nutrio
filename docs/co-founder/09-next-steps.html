Key Metrics Summary

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) = How much money we spend to get ONE new customer. Our CAC is 150 QAR (through referrals, word-of-mouth, not expensive ads).

LTV (Lifetime Value) = Total money a customer spends with us during their entire relationship. Our LTV is 8,400 QAR (average customer stays 12 months at 700 QAR/month).

LTV:CAC Ratio (56:1) = For every 1 QAR spent acquiring a customer, we earn 56 QAR back. Industry standard is 3:1. We are 18× more efficient!
