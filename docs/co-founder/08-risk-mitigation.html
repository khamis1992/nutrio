08 Risk & Mitigation

Key Risks & Our Solutions

High
Chicken-and-Egg (no restaurants = no users, no users = no restaurants)

Solution: Offer 0% commission for first 3 months to early restaurants. Build pipeline before launch.

High
Solo Founder Burnout

Solution: Hire Sales Lead by week 2 (fixed salary, no commission). Prioritize ruthlessly. Delegate everything except strategy.

Medium
Slow Restaurant Onboarding

Solution: Build 3x pipeline (150 restaurants vs 50 needed). Offer 0% commission incentive.

Medium
Low Retention

Solution: ONE subscription = access to ALL restaurants (variety prevents boredom). Get early feedback, iterate quickly.

Risk Reduction Strategy

  • Pre-Launch Restaurant Pipeline - Already identified 30-50 target restaurants
  • Low-CAC Acquisition - Start with referrals, community, restaurants (no expensive ads needed)
  • Data-Driven Iteration - PostHog analytics, weekly reviews, rapid learning
  • Brand Ambassador Program - Affiliate marketing for customer referrals (5-10% commission)
