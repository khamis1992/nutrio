The Problem

Qatar has 2.8 million residents. Health-conscious consumers face these problems:

  • Bored with same restaurant - Customers get bored ordering from the same restaurant every week with traditional meal subscriptions
  • No personalized nutrition - Existing apps offer food discovery but no nutrition tracking
  • Time-consuming meal planning - 73% of health-conscious consumers spend >2 hours/week planning meals
  • Lack of integration - Nutrition apps don't integrate with food delivery
🎯 Our Solution: ONE subscription gives customers access to ALL healthy restaurants! No more boredom — variety is built in.
