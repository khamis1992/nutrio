The Problem

Healthy Eating is Broken

For Consumers

  • No easy way to get healthy meals consistently
  • Expensive to eat healthy every day
  • Decision fatigue - what to eat?
  • Restaurants focus on taste, not nutrition
  • No subscription model for healthy eating

For Restaurants

  • High commissions (20-30%) from delivery apps
  • Slow payment (30-60 days)
  • No access to health-conscious customers
  • Commoditized in generic food delivery
  • No marketing support

The Solution

Subscription-Based Healthy Meal Platform

Pay Monthly · Get Meals Daily

For Consumers

  • Fixed monthly price for planned healthy meals
  • Choose from curated healthy restaurants
  • Delivery included
  • Nutrition tracking included
  • Flexible - pause or cancel anytime

For Restaurants

  • Only 18% commission (vs 20-30%)
  • Fast payment (7 days)
  • Access to health-conscious customers
  • Marketing & promotion support
  • Predictable order volume
