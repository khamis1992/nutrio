## Best Practices for Partner Content

1. **Rotate partners weekly** - Keep content fresh
2. **Get user feedback** - Share ratings/reviews
3. **Offer exclusive deals** - Partner discount codes
4. **Feature chef stories** - Humanize the brand
5. **Show quality process** - Transparency builds trust

---

## Engagement Tips

- **Tag partners** - They will share with their audience
- **Encourage reposts** - Partner tagging increases reach
- **Run joint giveaways** - Partner + Nutrio Fuel promo
- **Collect testimonials** - Feature in future posts
- **Create专属hashtags** - e.g., #NutrioPartnerGreenBowl
