## Best Practices for Nutrition Content

1. **Educate first, sell second** - Provide value before promoting
2. **Use simple visuals** - Complicated = ignored
3. **Make it actionable** - Tell them what to do
4. **Show real results** - Testimonials and progress
5. **Be supportive** - Encouraging tone, not judgmental
