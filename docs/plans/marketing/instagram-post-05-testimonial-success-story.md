## Best Practices for Testimonial Content

1. **Get permission** - Always ask before sharing
2. **Be authentic** - Real stories > perfect ones
3. **Include specifics** - Numbers, details = credibility
4. **Show variety** - Different demographics/goals
5. **Update regularly** - Fresh stories = active community

---

## Content Calendar: Testimonials

| Frequency | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| Weekly | Quick testimonial quote post |
| Bi-weekly | Full story carousel |
| Monthly | Video testimonial Reel |
| Quarterly | Community stats recap |

---

## Tips for Collecting Testimonials

1. **In-app prompts** - "Rate your experience"
2. **Post-delivery** - "How was your meal?"
3. **Social media** - Monitor mentions and DMs
4. **Email surveys** - 30-day follow-up
5. **Incentivize** - Offer discounts for reviews

---

## Legal Considerations

- ✅ Get written permission before posting
- ✅ Offer anonymity option
- ✅ Review testimonial for accuracy
- ✅ Comply with advertising regulations
- ✅ Include "Results may vary" if needed
