**Version**: 1.0

**Purpose**: Define co-founder roles, responsibilities, and team structure for 90-day launch phase

## Team Philosophy

### "You Build, Others Sell"

As technical founders, we focus on:
1. **Product**: Build the platform, ensure quality, scale infrastructure
2. **Business**: Sales, partnerships, growth, operations
3. **Execution**: Ship features, optimize processes, iterate quickly

**Delegation is Key**: Hire help for everything outside core strengths. Minimize burn, maximize velocity.

## Decision-Making Framework

### Daily Decisions
- **Owner**: Department lead (Technical/Business Founder)
- **Approval**: Co-founder consultation (12 hours max)
- **Escalation**: Founder if unresolved

### Weekly Decisions
- **Owner**: All founders (team agreement)
- **Approval**: Unanimous if major, majority if routine
- **Escalation**: None (weekly sync resolves)

### Monthly/KPI Decisions
- **Owner**: All founders
- **Approval**: Unanimous agreement
- **Process**: Monthly review meeting with data review

### Emergency Decisions
- **Owner**: First responder (technical or business)
- **Approval**: Notify other founder within 2 hours
- **Retro**: Review at next weekly meeting

## Equity Structure

### Proposed Split

| Role | Equity Range | Vesting | Notes |
|------|-------------|---------|-------|
| **Technical Co-Founder** | 25-35% | 4 years, 1-year cliff | Based on contribution |
| **Business Co-Founder** | 25-35% | 4 years, 1-year cliff | Based on contribution |
| **Founder (You)** | 20-30% | 4 years, 1-year cliff | Already building since start |
| **First Employee** | 0.5-2% | 4 years, 1-year cliff | Per hire (not all at once) |
| **Advisor Pool** | 2-5% | 2 years, 6-month cliff | For key advisors |
| **ESOP** | 10-15% | 4 years, 1-year cliff | For future hires (not active yet) |

### Vesting Details

**Standard Terms**:
- **Vesting Period**: 4 years
- **Cliff**: 1 year (0% before, 25% at 12 months)
- **Monthly Post-Cliff**: 1/48 of total per month
- **Acceleration**: Double trigger (change of control + termination)

**Equity Pool Allocation** (Post-Series A):
- Founders: 60-70% (combined)
- ESOP: 10-15%
- Investors: 15-20%
- Advisory: 2-5%

### Equity Adjustment Triggers

**Timing-Based Adjustments**:
- Pre-MVP (0-2 months): Higher equity (35% each)
- Post-MVP (2-4 months): Standard equity (30% each)
- Post-Traction (4-6 months): Reduced equity (25% each)
- Post-Scaling (6+ months): Negotiated (pro-rata based on contributions)

**Performance-Based Adjustments**:
- **Above Target**: +2-5% bonus equity (atFounder discretion)
- **Below Target**: -2-5% equity adjustment (with improvement plan)
- **Critical Blocker**: Equity review + action plan
