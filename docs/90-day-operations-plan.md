## Operations Playbook

### Restaurant Acquisition Playbook

**Target Restaurant Types**:
1. Health-focused (salad bars, juice shops, gluten-free)
2. New restaurants (still building clientele)
3. Delivery-native (already delivery-focused)
4. High-margin (can afford 18% commission)

**Onboarding Flow**:
1. Outreach (email, LinkedIn, in-person)
2. First call (30 minutes)
3. Demo (30 minutes)
4. Agreement (2-3 days)
5. Menu upload (1-2 days)
6. Training (1 hour)
7. Go-live

**Incentives**:
- 0% commission for first 3 months
- Featured placement for first 10
- Priority support for early adopters
- Revenue share for referrals

**Closing Rate Target**:
- Outreach: 100 restaurants
- First Call: 30% conversion (30 restaurants)
- Agreement: 30% conversion (9-10 restaurants)
- Go-Live: 90% conversion (9-10 active)

### Customer Acquisition Playbook

**Channels**:
1. **Referral Program** (Viral Coefficient target: 1.2+)
   - "Refer a friend, get free week"
   - Track via unique codes
   - Tiered rewards

2. **Restaurant Referrals** (Leverage partner network)
   - Ask restaurants to promote to customers
   - Revenue share: 5% on referred orders
   - Marketing materials provided

3. **Community Outreach** (Low CAC)
   - Qatar health/fitness Facebook groups
   - WhatsApp nutrition communities
   - Gym and fitness center partnerships

4. **Corporate Wellness** (B2B2C, high LTV)
   - Free pilot 1 month
   - QAR 50-100/employee/month
   - 20% discount for 50+ employees

**CAC Budget**: QAR 150 max per customer
**Target CAC Payback**: <30 days

### Customer Success Playbook

**First 7 Days**:
- Day 1: Welcome email + onboarding checklist
- Day 2: First meal reminder
- Day 3: nutrition goal check-in
- Day 5: Check-in survey
- Day 7: Success stories + invite to community

**Week 2-4**:
- Week 2: Progress celebration
- Week 3: Feedback loop
- Week 4: NPS survey + improvement plan

**Ongoing**:
- Weekly nutrition tips
- Monthly progress reports
- Quarterly wellness webinars
- Annual health assessment

### Restaurant Support Playbook

**Onboarding**:
- Welcome call → Menu upload → Training → Go-live
- Response time: <24 hours

**Ongoing Support**:
- Order issues: <1 hour response
- Menu questions: <4 hours response
- Payout questions: <24 hours response
- Monthly check-in calls

**Performance Tracking**:
- Menu completion rate >80%
- Order volume >20/week/restaurant
- Response time <2 hours
- Satisfaction >4.5/5
