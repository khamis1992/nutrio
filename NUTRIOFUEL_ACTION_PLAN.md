## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Risk Mitigation Strategies

#### 1. Competitive Risk

**Risk:** Major players (UberEats, DoorDash) enter health niche

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on differentiation (health personalization)
- Build strong brand loyalty
- Create switching costs (data, history, preferences)
- Develop exclusive restaurant partnerships
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High | **Mitigation Cost:** $50K

#### 2. Financial Risk

**Risk:** Cash flow constraints, unable to raise Series A

**Mitigation:**
- Conservative cash management (6-month runway)
- Milestone-based fundraising
- Multiple backup financing options
- Cost flexibility (variable vs fixed)
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High | **Mitigation Cost:** $100K (cash reserves)

#### 3. Operational Risk

**Risk:** Quality issues, delivery problems, partner disputes

**Mitigation:**
- Strict partner quality standards
- Automated quality monitoring
- Diversified delivery partnerships
- Customer satisfaction tracking
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium | **Mitigation Cost:** $75K

#### 4. Technology Risk

**Risk:** System outages, data breaches, technical debt

**Mitigation:**
- Robust infrastructure (cloud, backup)
- Security audits and penetration testing
- Insurance coverage
- Regular maintenance and updates
- **Probability:** Low | **Impact:** High | **Mitigation Cost:** $100K

#### 5. Team Risk

**Risk:** Key employee departure, inability to hire

**Mitigation:**
- Competitive compensation (salary + equity)
- Positive work culture
- Succession planning
- Retention bonuses
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium | **Mitigation Cost:** $150K
