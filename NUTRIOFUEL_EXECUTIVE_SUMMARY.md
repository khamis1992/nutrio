## 1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW

### 1.1 What We Do

NutriFuel operates as a multi-sided marketplace with three core value propositions:

**For Consumers:**
- Personalized meal recommendations based on health goals
- Convenient ordering from healthy restaurant options
- Nutrition tracking and progress monitoring
- Cost savings through subscription plans

**For Restaurants:**
- Access to health-conscious customer base
- Premium positioning and increased order volume
- Analytics and insights on customer preferences
- Marketing through featured listings

**For Partners:**
- Gyms, nutritionists, and wellness centers can monetize their audience
- Affiliate program with tiered commissions (2-10%)
- Premium analytics for business optimization

### 1.2 Market Opportunity

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $150B
- U.S. food delivery market: $150B (2025)
- Growing at 15% annually

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $30B
- Health-conscious segment (20% of TAM)
- Adults 25-45 focused on wellness

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $1.5B
- 5% market share in target cities
- 10 major markets over 5 years

## 3. REVENUE MODEL

### 3.1 Revenue Streams (Target Mix)

**1. Subscription Revenue (50% of total)**
- Basic: $49.99/week (5 meals)
- Pro: $99.99/week (14 meals)
- Premium: $149.99/week (unlimited)
- VIP Elite: $199.99/week (unlimited + 15% discount + perks)

**2. Platform Commissions (25%)**
- 15% commission on every order
- Auto-calculated via system trigger
- Configurable by restaurant tier

**3. Premium Analytics (10%)**
- Partner analytics dashboard
- Monthly/quarterly/yearly plans
- $29.99-249.99 pricing tiers

**4. Featured Listings (8%)**
- Weekly: $49

- High visibility placement

**5. Affiliate Program (5%)**
- Tiered commissions: 2-10%
- Performance bonuses
- Milestone rewards

**6. Promotional Partnerships (2%)**

- Health program integrations
